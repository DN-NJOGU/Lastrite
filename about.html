Lastrite: Honoring Legacies in the Digital Age

In an era where memories are increasingly captured and shared online, Lastrite emerges as a poignant solution for commemorating the lives of loved ones. Founded on the belief that every story deserves to be told and preserved, Lastrite is a pioneering company specializing in online obituaries.


Why Lastrite?

Gone are the days of traditional newspaper obituaries, confined to the pages of local publications. Lastrite brings the timeless practice of honoring the departed into the digital realm, offering a platform where families can create personalized tributes that reflect the uniqueness of each individual.
